School Laboratory

Was established in 1978

Our school has a science laboratory. Various experiments are performed by teacher, thus we get to learn visually many scientific things. It is really great opportunity for me to see such experiments. Experiments make our learning very interesting and explanatory.

School Library

Was build in 1980

Our school library is very spacious, well lighted and well ventilated. There is good sitting arrangement to read book in library.Our school library has variety of books, like story books, scientific information books, encyclopedia, mathematics book etc. We also get to read newspaper in our school. I enjoy reading story books in library period.

School computer lab

Was build in 2015

Our school computer lab is very spacious, well lighted and well ventilated. There are 10 computer systems in computer lab. I enjoy learning computer in school with my school mates.In computer class we are taught how to operate computer. I also learnt to to draw and paint in computer.

School Achievements

Inter School Table tennis tournament

In the year 2015 Wagle High School, Mangeshi won the Ponda taluka Under-14 boys school team table tennis tournament organised by Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs at Sports Complex, Ponda.

PONDA: Wagle High School, Mangeshi won the Ponda taluka Under-14 boys school team table tennis tournament organised by Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs at Sports Complex, Ponda, recently. In final, Wagle HS outplayed S.S. Samiti’s IVBD High School, Dhavali in straights sets 3-0. The team consisted of Atharv Nadkarni, Shreyash Shanbhag, Aniket Naik, Mohit Aiker and Switzel D’Souza. In the Under-14 girls tournament, the school secured second place at the taluka Level. The team consisted of Shruti Mardolkar, Rutuja Tendulkar, Alida Da Costa, Swejal Prabhugaonkar and Simran Velingkar. Sandeep Pagi, PE teacher, guided both the teams. Headmistress and the management congratulated all the players for their success.
